We are on a mission

Clarity

Email remains the #1 tool today’s workforce uses to communicate. Unfortunately, it was not designed for the ‘always on’ expectations that have become the norm. Thanks to Timyo, users can now benefit from clear and proactive communication.

Productivity

By helping you stay focused on important emails when they matter to you, Timyo helps you cut through the clutter. And when the people you work with use Timyo too, your email will come already prioritized by due date.

Peace

At Timyo, we believe it's time for email to make your life easier again. With email prioritized by time and features like Agenda View and Reply Tracker, Timyo takes the stress out of email and puts something much better in its place: peace.
